Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how Screen Reader Description ("SRD" or "the Extension") handles your information. Please read this policy carefully.

Data Collection and Usage

SRD does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data. We do not track your activity, gather information about your browsing habits, or store any data on our servers. Our extension operates entirely on your device and does not require any data to be shared with external servers.

Permissions

The only permissions requested by this Extension are necessary for its core functionality. These permissions are limited to modifying content on websites, and no data is retained by the extension or transmitted to any third party.

Cookies

This extension does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

SRD does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or store any of your personal or usage data.

Security

Since no personal or sensitive data is collected or stored, there are no specific security risks associated with this extension in relation to data protection. However, we recommend that you always keep your browser and operating system up to date for optimal security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
